About SkillChain

SkillChain is a student-driven learning platform designed to improve high school education through collaboration and technology.

Our Story

SkillChain was created by students who wanted better ways to learn, share, and support each other academically.

Our Vision

A world where students learn together, freely share knowledge, and grow beyond classroom limitations.
