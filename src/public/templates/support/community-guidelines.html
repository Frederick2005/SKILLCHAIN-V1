Community Guidelines

Rules and expectations for all SkillChain users.

Respect & Safety

  • Be respectful to all users
  • No hate speech, bullying, or harassment
  • Keep discussions educational and constructive

Content Rules

  • Only share original or properly credited content
  • No plagiarism or misleading information
  • Report harmful or inappropriate content

Enforcement

Violations may lead to content removal, account suspension, or permanent bans depending on severity.
